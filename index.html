The Thrun Lab

We are a team of expert computer scientists with the singular aim of significantly helping society through artificial intelligence technologies, and are constantly on the lookout for high-impact projects.

We have worked on robotics, self-driving cars, automated homes, healthcare, drones, and a number of other applications.

We currently focus on three areas: AI for healthcare, AI for people-prediction, and smart-homes.

Recent Work - Nature

In this work, highlighted on the cover of the journal Nature, we demonstrate that a deep learning system can match the performance of board-certified dermatologists at skin cancer classification using photographic and dermoscopic images. Our system generalizes well, and works for both malignant melanoma and keratinocyte carcinoma identification.
